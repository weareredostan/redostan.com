SELF CONSTRUCTIONISM

Self-Constructionists are "Agents of Development".

Individuals can choose to develop their own ideas.

The best of ideas will improve the lives of others.

Selflessness is the main identifiable feature of an "Agent of Development".

We are creators.
You live in your own reality.

Your intentions shape your environment. Your perspective is your experience.

Self-Reflection ultimately leads to the path of a healer.
Knowing yourself is how you can know others.

Your internal climb towards balance will grant you tools and awareness necessary to analyze outside of you.
